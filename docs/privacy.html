Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

No Data Collection

Stampbox is an offline-first app. All the information you enter is stored locally on your device in the app's own sandboxed storage. We do not collect, transmit, or have access to any of your data. There are no analytics, no tracking pixels, and no third-party SDKs that phone home.

No Account Required

The app does not require you to create an account, and we do not collect your name, email, or any personal identifiers.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed entirely by Apple's StoreKit. We never see your payment information; Apple's own privacy policy governs that transaction.

Changes

If this policy changes, the update will be posted on this page.
